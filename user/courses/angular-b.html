AngularJS Expressions

Expressions inside {{ }} automatically bind data to the view and can perform simple calculations.

5 + 5 = {{ 5 + 5 }}

Double of {{expr.x || 0}} is {{ (expr.x || 0) * 2 }}

<div>
  <p>5 + 5 = {{ 5 + 5 }}</p>
</div>

AngularJS Modules

Modules group controllers, services, and components into a single app structure:

var app = angular.module('myApp', []);
