How EduHub Works

EduHub makes managing education simple and efficient for institutions, teachers, and students.

Institutions Register

Schools and colleges create their profiles, set up courses, and onboard faculty members with ease.

Teachers Manage Classes

Teachers can create and manage schedules, upload assignments, and communicate with students directly.

Students Enroll & Learn

Students register for courses, track their progress, and submit assignments in an easy-to-use platform.

Admins Monitor Progress

Administrators get full access to analytics dashboards, ensuring smooth academic and administrative operations.

Secure Cloud Access

All data is encrypted and stored securely in the cloud, ensuring safe access anytime, anywhere.
